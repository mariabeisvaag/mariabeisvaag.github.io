How to play (30 seconds)

Work together as a team — guessing is allowed!
  1. 🕵️ Be tech detectives: talk together and make your best guess.
  2. Package 1: Communication uses decades (e.g., 1980s).
  3. Package 2: Gaming & creativity uses exact years (e.g., 2007).
  4. Press Start to begin the timer (no stress!).
  5. Drag each card from the pile up to the correct point on the timeline.
  6. Each timeline point can hold one card. Dropping a new card replaces the old one.
  7. When all cards are placed, press Check score for a big reveal 🎉
